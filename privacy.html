Privacy Policy

Your records,
kept private.

This policy explains how Fiona handles data and what information is shared with third parties.

What we store

Weight logs, body measurements, and your in-app preferences are stored on your device.

iCloud sync (optional)

When iCloud is available and enabled on your device, Fiona can sync your records through your private Apple iCloud account using CloudKit. We do not run our own database for your personal body records and we cannot read your private iCloud container data.

Purchases and subscriptions

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple. Subscription state is managed in-app with RevenueCat. These services process billing-related data needed to validate purchases and restore access.

Support messages

If you contact support, we only receive the information you choose to send in your email or message.
